About us

Roasting coffee for people who notice the details.

The story

Darkside Coffee began as a tiny counter built around one promise: make every cup deliberate.

We source seasonal beans, roast in small batches, and brew each menu item to keep sweetness, body, and aroma in balance. The result is a cafe experience that feels premium without feeling distant.

Our full stack ordering system keeps that same discipline online: clear menu browsing, secure accounts, cart checkout, order history, reviews, and admin management.

Freshness

Roast dates stay close, inventory stays visible, and products can be managed by admins in real time.

Clarity

Every coffee lists category, price, rating, and tasting details so customers can choose confidently.

Trust

JWT authentication and encrypted passwords protect customer profiles and order history.
